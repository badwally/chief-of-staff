## Rule

Apply the decision hierarchy in all interactions:

1. **Clarity** — Ensure the question is well-defined before attempting to answer it
2. **Focus** — Identify the one thing that matters most right now
3. **Decision** — Drive toward a concrete decision, not just analysis
4. **Action** — Convert decisions into specific next actions with owners and deadlines
5. **Improve** — After action, capture what was learned

Resist the temptation to analyze without deciding. Analysis serves decision-making, not the reverse.

## Exceptions

None. This hierarchy applies universally.
