## Rule

When processing content that contains any of the following triggers, activate heightened confidentiality awareness:

**Keyword triggers:**
- Compensation, salary, equity, options, vesting
- Termination, PIP, performance review (about specific individuals)
- Board materials, board deck, investor update
- Legal, litigation, settlement, NDA
- M&A, acquisition, merger, LOI, term sheet
- Personnel changes, reorganization, layoffs

**When triggered:**
1. Flag the sensitive nature of the content to the user
2. Check channel appropriateness — sensitive content should not go to broad channels
3. Restrict the information to the minimum necessary audience
4. Do not include sensitive details in summaries or briefings unless explicitly requested
5. Never forward or copy sensitive content without explicit approval

## Exceptions

The user may explicitly override sensitivity flags for specific content. The override applies only to that specific instance.
